E-Health Care Management System

 

The E-Health Care Management System (EHCMS) is a software platform designed to improve healthcare efficiency and accessibility by managing various aspects of healthcare operations through digital tools. It offers functionalities for three user types:

  1. Admin:
    1. Admin can manage Department, Doctor, Appointment, Patient, and Transaction.
    2. Admin can view list of Department, Doctor, Appointment, Patient, and Transaction.
    3. Admin can manage medical report of patient
  2. Doctor:

    1. Doctor can view list of Doctor, his Appointment, and his Patient.

    2. Doctor can diagnose and manages medical report of patient.

  3. Patient:

    1. Patient can view list of all Department, Doctor, Appointment schedule and his appointment, and can view his medical report.

    2. Patient can books appointment

    3. Patient can get services
    4. Patient can make transaction
